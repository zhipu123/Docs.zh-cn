## <a name="log-for-insight"></a><span data-ttu-id="af796-175">要深入探索日志</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-175">Log for insight</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="af796-176">遥测包是良好的第一步，但你仍必须检测你自己的代码。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-176">A telemetry package is a good first step, but you still have to instrument your own code.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="af796-177">遥测服务告知你问题时，并告诉您什么遇到客户，但它可能不为你提供大量的深入了解发生了什么情况在代码中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-177">The telemetry service tells you when there's a problem and tells you what the customers are experiencing, but it may not give you a lot of insight into what's going on in your code.</span></span>

### <a name="log-in-production"></a><span data-ttu-id="af796-182">在生产中记录</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-182">Log in production</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="af796-183">许多人开启在生产环境中的跟踪，仅当没有问题，他们想要调试时。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-183">A lot of people turn on tracing in production only when there's a problem and they want to debug.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="af796-184">这可以大幅之间加入延迟你发现问题的时间和获取有关它的有用疑难解答信息的时间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-184">This can introduce a substantial delay between the time you're aware of a problem and the time you get useful troubleshooting information about it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="af796-185">并且你获取的信息可能不是有用的间歇性的错误。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af796-185">And the information you get might not be helpful for intermittent errors.</span></span>
